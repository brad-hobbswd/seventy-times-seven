About the Author

Bradley Hobbs writes with a focus on healing, identity, and spiritual transformation through Jesus. His work centers on helping people walk through real pain without losing faith, while discovering the authority and freedom found in Christ.

His writing speaks to those who have experienced silence, rejection, or misunderstanding, offering clarity, truth, and restoration through the presence of Jesus.

Seven Times Seventy reflects a journey of forgiveness, healing, and spiritual authority lived out in real experiences.
